Computer Science is the study of computers and computational systems, including their theory, design, development, and application. It covers a wide range of topics, such as:  

- **Algorithms & Data Structures** – How to efficiently process and store data.  
- **Programming Languages** – Writing instructions for computers in languages like Python, C++, or Java.  
- **Computer Architecture** – Understanding how hardware components interact.  
- **Operating Systems** – Managing hardware and software resources.  
- **Databases** – Organizing and retrieving large amounts of data efficiently.  
- **Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning** – Creating smart systems that learn from data.  
- **Computer Networks & Cybersecurity** – Ensuring safe and efficient communication between systems.  
- **Theory of Computation** – Studying what problems can be solved by computers and how efficiently.
